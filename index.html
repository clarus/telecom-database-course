SD202 - Databases

May - June 2020

Databases have been the central part of all information system for many years. Appeared in the eighties, the relational database systems have never stopped to evolve. The goal of this class is to understand the main ideas with which the databases are though, implemented and used. It focuses on relational databases as this certainly is the most mature technology for databases today.
