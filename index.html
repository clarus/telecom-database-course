SD202 - Databases - 2021

April - June 2021

Databases have been the central part of all information system for many years. Appeared in the eighties, the relational database systems have never stopped to evolve. The goal of this class is to understand the main ideas with which the databases are though, implemented and used. It focuses on relational databases as this may be one of the most used kind of databases today.
